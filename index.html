Brick by Brick

Deconstructing the Data of LEGO's Official Sets

LEGO is considered the most popular toy brand in the world and is loved by children and adults alike.

Rebrickable is a website where users can buy and sell LEGOs. It is perhaps one of the largest second-hand LEGO marketplaces.

Using their inventory data, we are able to extrapolate some interesting insights about the LEGO franchise. What are the most popular brick colors? Which set has the most pieces? What are some of the most common LEGO set themes? Let's find out.
